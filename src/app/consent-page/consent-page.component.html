Study Information Sheet

Design and Evaluation of Data-Driven Stickers on Social Media

Lead Researcher Daniel Epstein, Ph.D. Assistant Professor, Department of Informatics 949-824-4197, epstein@ics.uci.edu

  • Please read the information below carefully. If you have any comments, concerns, or questions regarding this study, please contact the researcher listed above.
  • You are being asked to participate in a research study. Participation in this study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate or discontinue your involvement at any time without penalty or loss of benefits. You are free to withdraw from this study at any time.
  • At a societal level, this research will inform the design of new social media features which aim to help people receive the kinds of support they desire online.
  • To participate in this study, you must be at least 18 years old and send or receive at least one Snap on Snapchat per week, on average.
  • We would like you to complete a survey to gather your feedback on the design of stickers driven by data people often collect themselves, posted on ephemeral social media content like Snapchat or Instagram Stories. The survey will last about 10 minutes.
  • Some survey questions pertain to your attitude toward online personal information disclosure scenarios. These questions may induce a feeling of discomfort.
  • All research data collected will be stored securely and confidentially in cloud storage. Only the research team will have access to the data.
  • Your information collected as part of this research will not be used or distributed for future research studies.
  • You will receive $2 via Amazon for your participation in this study.
  • If you have questions or concerns about your rights as a research participant, you can contact the UCI Institutional Review Board by phone, (949) 824-6662, by e-mail at IRB@research.uci.edu or at 141 Innovation, Suite 250, Irvine, CA 92697.
